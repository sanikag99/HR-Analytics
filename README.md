# HR-Analytics
📊 HR Analytics Dashboard
🔹 Short Description / Purpose

The HR Analytics Dashboard is an interactive Power BI report designed to analyze employee data and identify key factors influencing attrition. It helps HR teams understand workforce trends and make data-driven decisions to improve employee retention and performance.

🛠️ Tech Stack
📊 Power BI Desktop – Dashboard development and visualization
📂 Power Query – Data cleaning and transformation
🧠 DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Calculations and KPIs
🔗 Data Modeling – Relationships between HR data fields
📁 File Format – .pbix and .csv
📁 Data Source

The dataset consists of employee-level HR data with 1470 records, including attributes such as age, salary, job role, education, and attrition status. The data is structured to enable analysis of workforce demographics, compensation, and employee turnover patterns.

🌟 Features / Highlights
📌 Business Problem

Employee attrition is a major challenge for organizations, leading to increased hiring costs and productivity loss. However, identifying the root causes of attrition across different employee segments is difficult using raw data.

Key questions include:

Which employees are most likely to leave?
How do salary and experience impact attrition?
Which job roles and departments have the highest turnover?
🎯 Goal of the Dashboard

To build an interactive analytics tool that:

Tracks and monitors employee attrition
Identifies high-risk employee groups
Enables department-wise analysis
Supports strategic HR decision-making
📊 Walkthrough of Key Visuals
Key KPIs
Displays total employees (1470), attrition count (237), attrition rate (16.1%), average age (37), average salary (6.5K), and average years at company (7).
Attrition by Education (Donut Chart)
Shows highest attrition among Life Sciences and Medical backgrounds.
Attrition by Age (Bar Chart)
Highlights that employees aged 26–35 have the highest attrition.
Attrition by Salary Slab (Bar Chart)
Indicates most attrition occurs in the lowest salary group (below 5K).
Attrition by Years at Company (Line Chart)
Shows higher attrition in early years of employment.
Attrition by Job Role (Bar Chart)
Identifies roles like Laboratory Technician and Sales Executive with high turnover.
Department Filter (Slicer)
Allows users to analyze HR, R&D, and Sales departments individually.
📈 Business Impact & Insights
Improved Retention Strategy: Helps identify employees at high risk of leaving
Compensation Insights: Highlights the link between low salary and high attrition
Workforce Planning: Identifies departments and roles with high turnover
Data-Driven Decisions: Enables proactive HR strategies instead of reactive actions
